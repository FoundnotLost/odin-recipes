Ingredient Checklist ¾ cup all-purpose flour, divided ⅔ cup beer 1 large egg 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder 2 cups flaked coconut 24 medium raw shrimp, shelled, deveined, with tails attached 3 cups oil for frying Directions Instructions Checklist Step 1 Line a baking sheet with wax paper; set aside. Step 2 Whisk together 1/2 cup flour, beer, egg, and baking powder in a medium bowl until smooth. Place coconut and remaining 1/4 cup flour in 2 separate bowls. Step 3 One at a time, hold shrimp by the tail and dredge in flour, shaking off excess. Dip in beer batter; allow excess to drip off. Roll in coconut, then place on the prepared baking sheet. Refrigerate for 30 minutes. Step 4 Heat oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Step 5 Working in batches, fry shrimp in hot oil, turning once, until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes. Use tongs to remove shrimp to paper towels to drain. Home
